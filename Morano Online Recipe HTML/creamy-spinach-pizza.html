Creamy Spinach Pizza

Spinach on pizza may sound weird, but for those who know about this awesome pizza topping from Angel’s Pizza, you know it’s a combination that you can only taste to fall in love with!

Making Time:

  • Prep: 20 minutes
  • Cook: 10 hour

Ingredients:

  • 3 bundle kangkong (water spinach) leaves and tender stalks only.
  • 6 tablespoons Oil
  • 1.5 medium white onion peeled, sliced thinly
  • 6 cloves Garlic chopped
  • 3 226-gram block cream cheese softened
  • 6 190-gram blocks mozzarella cheese grated
  • 6 10-inch pizza crusts
  • Salt and Ground Black Pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse kangkong leaves. Place in a salad spinner to dry. Roughly chop. Set aside.

  2. In a nonstick pan over medium heat, heat oil. Add garlic and cook until almost toasted. Add onions. Cook onions until softened, about 5 to 10 minutes, lowering heat as needed to prevent browning. Add kangkong leaves and stir. Cook until wilted, about 3 minutes. Season with salt and ground black pepper to taste. Remove from heat.

  3. Transfer spinach mixture into a large bowl. Add cream cheese, mayonnaise, and Parmesan cheese. Stir to combine.

  4. Preheat oven to 200 degrees C or 400 degrees F. Place rack on the bottom shelf.

  5. Place prepared pizza crust onto a baking tray. Divide spinach dip into balls the size of ping pong balls (about 1-inch rounds) and place in evenly divided points around the pizza. Cover the pizza with mozzarella cheese.

  6. Slide the pizza onto the bottom rack. Bake in the preheated oven for 5 minutes or until the mozzarella has melted and the bottom of the crust is browned. Remove from the oven, slice, and serve immediately.

Serving: 4-6 people
